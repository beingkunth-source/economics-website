India – Selected Economic Concepts

India is one of the largest and fastest-changing economies in the world. This page connects core economic ideas with examples related to India.

Photo by Pixabay – Pexels

Structure of the Economy

Primary Sector

Activities based on natural resources such as agriculture, forestry, fishing and mining.

Secondary Sector

Manufacturing and industry, where raw materials are converted into finished goods.

Tertiary Sector

Services such as trade, transport, finance, information technology, education and health care.

Useful Official Sources

  • Reserve Bank of India (RBI) – monetary policy, banking statistics and reports.
  • Ministry of Statistics and Programme Implementation (MoSPI) – GDP, prices, employment and many other official data series.
  • Economic Survey of India – annual review of the economy published by the Ministry of Finance.
